<p>A review preference is a small integer that indicates how much you want to evaluate an artifact. Positive numbers mean you want to review the artifact, negative numbers mean you don’t, and −100 means you think you have a conflict or do not have an access to required hardware or software. −20 to 20 is a typical range for real preferences; multiple papers can have the same preference. The automatic assignment algorithm attempts to assign reviews in descending preference order. Different users’ preference values are not compared and need not use the same scale.</p> <p>The list shows all submitted artifacts. Select a column heading to sort by that column. Enter preferences in the text boxes or on the artifact pages. You may also upload preferences from a text file; see the “Download” and “Upload” links below the paper list.</p>...
